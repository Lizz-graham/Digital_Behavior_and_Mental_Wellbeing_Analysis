Mental health and digital wellbeing analysis (2020–2024)

Objective:
Exploring the relationship between digital behavior and overall mental health.

Dataset:
500 observations across the following:
- daily screen time (min) on phone/desktop etc 
- number of app switches
- sleep(hrs), (self reported)
- notification count
- social media usage (min)
- focus (self reported score from 1–10, where 1 is very distracted and 10 is highly focused)
- mood (self reported score from 1–10, where 1 is very poor mood and 10 is excellent mood)
- anxiety level (self reported score from 1–10, where 1 is low anxiety and 10 is high anxiety
- digital wellbeing score (calculated score based on focus, sleep, and anxiety levels; higher value represents better well-being)
